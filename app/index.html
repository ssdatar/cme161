Unicorns of the World - where and how big?

This visualization tells the stories of the unicorn startups around the world, where they are based, and what are the top valued companies. It uses C3.js to build the charts. The source code for C3.js, which is a wrapper library over D3.js to generate quick charts, can be found here.

The Targaryen Family Tree

This visualization shows the family tree of the Targaryens, a powerful family from Game of Thrones. Game of Thrones is a hugely popular show adapted from A Song of Ice and Fire, a fantasy novel series written by George RR Martin. The show is full of complex families, and this visualization helps keep track of who belongs to which family. Especially with this [SPOILER ALERT] theory. The library used is D3.js.

Baseball statistics using Crossfilter
